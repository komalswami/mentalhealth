Depression and Anxiety - Overview

Depression is a mood disorder that affects mood, thinking, feeling as well as the behavior of an affected person. The affected person may have trouble doing his daily activities. Some people experience these feelings very intensely, some experience it for long periods of time (weeks, months or even years) and sometimes without any apparent reason. This disorder is also known as major depressive disorder (Major Depression) or clinical depression.

Depression Causes and Treatment

Depression causes vary from person to person.
